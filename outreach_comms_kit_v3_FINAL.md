**When:** Day 1, before any call is made

**Subject:** Internship Opportunities for MBA Students - Tarcin

---

Tarcin is a product development company building technology platforms across robotics, EdTech, automation, and digital learning systems.

1. Short-term internship aligned with college requirements
2. Six-month structured internship pathway listed on the AICTE Internship Portal

If this may be relevant for your students, I would be grateful for a brief opportunity to share the details and understand the suitable next steps.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
Tarcin
Tarcin Robotic LLP
[Mobile] | [Email]
https://tarcin.in

---

**Notes:**
- No PDF. No form link. Keep this as a simple introduction before the call.
- Goal is simple: introduce Tarcin clearly and request a brief conversation.
- If they reply to this email before you call -- great. Call them immediately.

## PIECE 1 - TPO Phone Call Script (60 Seconds Max)

**Channel:** Phone call
**When:** Day 3 -- after the intro email has been sent
**Caller:** Team member assigned to that college

---

**Opening:**

"Good morning Sir / Ma'am. Am I speaking with [Name] from [College Name]?"

(If gatekeeper: "Could you connect me with the Training and Placement Officer for the MBA department please?")

---

**Main pitch (30 seconds):**

"I am [Your Name] from Tarcin. I sent you a brief email a couple of days ago about internship opportunities for your MBA students.

Tarcin is currently building technology products across robotics, EdTech, automation, and digital learning systems. For this batch, we are opening a few internship slots for MBA students to work on live product, marketing, analytics, customer engagement, and program coordination activities.

I wanted to check whether this may be relevant for students from [College Name]."

---

**Ask for WhatsApp (not another email):**

"May I send you the full details on WhatsApp? Much quicker than email."

---

**If they say "Send an email":**

"Of course -- should I resend to the college inbox or directly to your number so it does not get missed?"

(Redirect to WhatsApp. Email is fallback, not goal.)

---

**If they say "Visit us":**

"Absolutely. Before I come, may I send you a one-page overview on WhatsApp so your time is well spent?"

(Do not visit cold. Confirm interest first.)

---

**If they show interest:**

"That is great. I will send everything right now. Would [specific day and time] work for a 15-minute follow-up call?"

---

**Must-mention checklist for every call:**
- Lead with Tarcin's live product work and current internship requirement
- Mention TSM / Velammal only if credibility is needed during the conversation
- "Internship slots for this batch" -- not "we are looking for interns"
- AICTE-listed -- colleges usually prefer structured and recognized internship listings
- Get WhatsApp number before ending the call
- Confirm a specific next step before hanging up -- do not leave it vague

## PIECE 5 - Event Invite (Day 10 - Last Touch Before Parking)

**Channel:** WhatsApp or email
**When:** Day 10, if college has not responded to any previous touch
**Purpose:** Shift the conversation from internship to event -- lower-commitment yes

---

Dear [Name],

We are conducting a statewide technology challenge for MBA and engineering students across Tamil Nadu -- similar to the Madurai Tech Cup we ran earlier this year.

We would like [College Name] to be listed as an official participating institution. It is free for students, your college gets visibility across all event materials, and students receive participation certificates.

No paperwork required to participate. I can share a one-pager with full details.

Would this be of interest?

[Your Name] | Tarcin | [Mobile]

---

**Notes:**
- This is a relationship opener -- not a sales pitch
- If they say yes to the event, the internship conversation becomes much warmer next semester
- Do not mention internship in this message -- one thing at a time
