# Tarcin MBA Internship Campaign - Resource Responsibility Matrix

## Purpose

This matrix defines the resource roles required to execute the MBA internship outreach phase. The goal is to assign responsibilities by function first, then map available people to the right roles.

---

## Role Matrix

| Role | Primary Responsibility | Key Activities | Daily Output | Skills Needed | Suggested Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Campaign Owner | Own the full campaign outcome | Approve strategy, unblock team, decide priority colleges, approve visits, review daily progress | Daily decision notes and blockers cleared | Decision-making, authority, campaign understanding | Founder / Senior Lead |
| College Outreach Lead | Own college-side communication | Send intro emails, call TPOs, send WhatsApp follow-ups, schedule calls/visits, capture responses | Colleges contacted, call outcomes, next follow-ups | Strong communication, Tamil + English, confidence on calls | To assign |
| Student Acquisition Coordinator | Own student-side interest and conversion | Monitor Google Form, contact interested students, explain internship pathways, separate short-term and AICTE applicants | Applications received, students contacted, qualified student list | Fast response, student-friendly communication, WhatsApp handling | To assign |
| Content & Collateral Owner | Own all campaign assets | Finalize one-page overview, flyer, Google Form text, QR code, email/WhatsApp copy, keep latest versions clean | Updated assets and links ready for use | Writing, design sense, detail orientation | To assign |
| CRM / Tracker Owner | Own data discipline and daily status | Maintain tracker, update stage/status, check missing contacts, assign follow-up dates, prepare daily summary | Updated tracker and daily status report | Excel/Zoho comfort, accuracy, follow-up discipline | To assign |
| Visit / Partnership Coordinator | Own in-person or formal partnership follow-up | Prepare visit agenda, coordinate meeting time, carry overview/MOU if needed, document commitments | Visit schedule, meeting notes, closure next steps | Professional presence, coordination, institutional communication | Senior team member |

## Minimum Team Model

If only 3 people are available, combine roles like this:

| Person | Combined Role | Responsibilities |
|---|---|---|
| Person 1 | College Outreach Lead | Emails, calls, WhatsApp follow-ups, visit scheduling |
| Person 2 | Student Acquisition Coordinator | Student applications, student WhatsApp follow-up, applicant qualification |
| Person 3 | CRM / Tracker + Content Owner | Tracker, daily report, Google Form, one-page overview, flyer links |

Campaign Owner should remain separate if possible.

## Daily Stand-Up Questions

1. Which colleges moved forward today?
2. Which colleges need follow-up tomorrow?
3. Which students applied today?
4. Which students are ready for onboarding discussion?
5. Which asset/link is blocking outreach?
6. Which college should be parked or escalated?

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Not Started | No action taken yet |
| Intro Email Sent | PIECE 0 sent |
| Call Attempted | At least one call made |
| Connected | TPO or relevant person spoken to |
| WhatsApp Sent | Follow-up details shared on WhatsApp |
| Post-Call Email Sent | Full details shared after positive call |
| Interested | College showed clear interest |
| Visit Scheduled | Meeting date fixed |
| Applications Received | At least one student applied |
| Active | College is moving through process |
| Parked | No response or not relevant this semester |
| Closed | Outreach complete for this cycle |
