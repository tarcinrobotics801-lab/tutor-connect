# Tarcin MBA Internship - Student Application and Onboarding Flow

## Purpose

This document captures the student-side process for the MBA internship campaign. It covers how students move from application to WhatsApp group, orientation, selection, and onboarding.

This is a review draft and can be updated after team discussion.

---

## Overall Student Funnel

```text
Flyer / College Share / WhatsApp Message
        ↓
Google Form Application
        ↓
Form Confirmation Message with WhatsApp Group Link
        ↓
Student Joins WhatsApp Group
        ↓
Group Updates + Orientation
        ↓
Role Preference Confirmation
        ↓
Shortlisting
        ↓
Selection
        ↓
Onboarding
        ↓
First-Week Task Assignment
```

---

## Core Principle

The Google Form should not only collect student data. It should act as the entry point into the student communication funnel.

Instead of manually messaging every applicant, students should be guided to join the official WhatsApp group immediately after submitting the form.

This reduces manual follow-up and helps the team communicate updates, orientation details, role expectations, and next steps in one place.
